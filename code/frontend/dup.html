For You

Personalized recommendations built from your listening taste

How it works: Every time you ❤️ a track, Wavelength reads that track's CLAP audio embedding — a 512-dimensional fingerprint of its sound — and updates your personal taste vector as a running average of all your likes. When you open this tab, it searches the FAISS index of 106,000+ tracks for the nearest neighbors to your taste vector, so the more you like, the better your picks get.
